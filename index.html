About Us

Started in 2003, we are successfully fast in catching up to be a market player with background of the personnel experience and expertise in the business, and our long-time cooperation with the strong existing team and reliable supplier.

Servicing Indonesia’ market by representing and selling well-known brand in carpet and resilient flooring, and famous design-oriented furniture brand from around the world, we are proud with our on-time delivery and professional installer team.

Add to that, since 2016 we have our own furniture factory in Bitung, producing high quality furniture using high end imported materials and state of the art machinery, a quality meets imported furniture standard with fast local delivery.

We satisfied clients in their offices and working spaces and in education segment such as schools, healthcare segment such as hospitals, hospitality segment such as hotel and restaurant, and public service segment such as airport and library.
